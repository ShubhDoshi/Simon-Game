How to play Simon Game?

  • Press any key on your keyboard to start.

  • A new button will light up at each level.

  • Adding this newly lighted button to your previous sequence will now form your correct answer sequence.

  • After that, you need to press the buttons in a correct sequential order right from the beginning.

  • If you correctly press the buttons, then you will advance to the next level.

  • If at any stage your sequence doesn't match with the correct answer sequence, then you will lose and will have to restart the game.

  • Play and enjoy the game!
